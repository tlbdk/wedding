Your stay in Riga

For all our international guests who would choose to spend some time in Riga, we would recommend Astor Riga Hotel & Conference.

It is freshly renovated, conveniently located in the Old Town of Riga, and a double room can be booked for under 100 EUR per night (provided booking well in advance, so please do not delay making your reservations). This will also be the departure / arrival location of the chartered bus to Jumurda.

Please note, accommodation is provided for all our guests for the night of Saturday, August 15 in the Jumurda Manor House.
